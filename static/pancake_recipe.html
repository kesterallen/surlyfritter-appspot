Homemade Cafe Style Buttermilk Pancakes

(Bette's recipie + 30g flour, all buttermilk, no sugar, half butter)


By @EastBayPancakes.

Ingredients

  • 300 g flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs separated, whites whipped to soft peaks
  • 2 Tbsp butter melted and cooled
  • 2.5 cups buttermilk

Instructions

  • Sift dry ingredients together and set aside
  • Mix buttermilk, butter, and egg yolks together
  • Gently fold wet ingredients into dry, do not overmix
  • Gently fold whites into mixture
  • Rest batter for 10 miniutes
  • Gently scoop 1/2 - 1 cup batter per pancake onto a buttered griddle
  • Batter will not bubble through, so be careful when to flip
