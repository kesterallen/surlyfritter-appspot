Sourdough Buttermilk Pancakes / Waffles

(skip baking soda? It didn't mix in well)


Modified from NY Times Cooking {% endblock %} {% block recipe %}

Ingredients

For the overnight sponge, or base:

  • 1 cup / 240 grams unfed sourdough starter
  • 1 cup / 224 grams buttermilk
  • 1 cup / 120 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar

For the batter:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

  • Put the sourdough starter in a large bowl.
  • Add the buttermilk, flour, and sugar, then stir to combine.
  • Cover the bowl and allow it to rest overnight at room temperature.
  • When you are ready to cook, whisk the egg and melted butter together in a bowl, then add the rested sponge.
  • Add the salt and the baking soda to the batter and mix thoroughly to combine.
  • Pour some of the batter onto a preheated greased waffle iron, or make pancakes
