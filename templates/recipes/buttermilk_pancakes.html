Homemade Cafe -Style Buttermilk Pancakes

(Bette's recipe + 30g flour, all buttermilk, no sugar, half butter)


By @EastBayPancakes.
{% endblock %} {% block ingredients%}

Ingredients

  • 300 g flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs separated, whites whipped to soft peaks (whipping the whites is a pain, and probably doubles the work, but it's what takes these to the next level)
  • 2 Tbsp butter melted and cooled
  • 2.5 cups buttermilk, pref room temp
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • Sift dry ingredients together and set aside
  • Whip egg whites to soft peaks
  • Mix buttermilk, butter, and egg yolks together
  • Gently fold wet ingredients into dry, do not overmix
  • Gently fold egg whites into mixture
  • Rest batter for 10 miniutes
  • Gently scoop 1/2 - 1 cup batter per pancake onto a medium-hot buttered griddle
  • Carefully turn over pancake when bubbles appear on top. Batter will not bubble through much since it is extra-thick

Options

  • Substitue one cup of yogurt for one of the cups of buttermilk for a firmer pancake with a crispier top and bottom
